How This Calculator Works

This calculator estimates your take-home pay after federal and Michigan state taxes using simplified assumptions. Federal tax is calculated using a basic estimated rate, while Michigan applies a flat income tax rate.

Michigan State Income Tax Overview (2026)

Michigan applies a flat state income tax rate to taxable income. Unlike progressive systems used in some states, the same percentage applies across income levels.

Federal vs State Taxes

Federal income taxes are progressive and vary by filing status and income bracket. Michigan uses a flat tax structure, which simplifies state tax calculations.
