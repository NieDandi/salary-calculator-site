Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my salary after tax?

To calculate your take-home pay, subtract federal income tax, state income tax, Social Security, and Medicare from your gross salary. Our calculator performs these calculations instantly.

Which states have no income tax?

Some US states do not charge state income tax. However, federal taxes and payroll taxes still apply.

Yes, this calculator is designed using updated tax structures for 2026 to provide reliable salary estimates.
