How New Jersey Income Tax Works

In New Jersey, state income tax is applied in addition to federal income tax. While federal tax rates are progressive across the United States, New Jersey applies its own state-level income tax rules. For estimation purposes, this calculator uses an approximate state tax rate of 0.07. Actual tax liability may vary depending on deductions, credits, and filing status.
